Static Centrifugal Pump

Inherits from : Basic Transport Model

TSP Handbook : Chapter 12 (p. 312)

Description

The module “Static Centrifugal Pump” is one of the major components of the secondary circuit, as it converts the mechanical power contained into the steam into hydraulic power.
