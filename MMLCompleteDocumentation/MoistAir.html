Moist Air

Moist air is used instead of FlueGases when the temperatures are negative, because the medium used by FlueGases cannot handle negative temperatures when there is water in the gas mixture.

The MoistAir medium is provided by Modelica Open Library.

All moist air components extends common components.

There is a converter to go from moist air to flue gases.

MoistAirToFlueGases

This component is used to convert the medium from MoistAir to FlueGases.

It is made of one sink for the moist air (left side) and one source for flue gases (right side).
