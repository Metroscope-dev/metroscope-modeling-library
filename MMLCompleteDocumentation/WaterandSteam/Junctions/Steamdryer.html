Steam dryer

TSP handbook : Section 14.9 page 442

Wet steam is steam containing some quantity of water. Wet steam can reduce the plant efficiency and cause damage to most items and equipment of the plant. The steam dryer is a reservoir of steam and water used to separate water from the steam generated in the boiler.

The steam dryer has 3 connectors : one inlet and two outlets : one for vapor and one for liquid vapor. Inside, it is composed of two BasicTransportModels.

Mechanical balance

There is no pressure loss modeled in the steam extraction dryer, therefore the pressure at the inlet is the same as the pressure at both outlets.
