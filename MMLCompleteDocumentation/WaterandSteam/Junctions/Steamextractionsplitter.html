Steam extraction splitter

The module “Steam Extraction Splitter” represents the extraction of a small fraction of the steam passing through the turbine used to supply a heater. The moisture content of the steam extracted can differ from the one in the main steam.

The steam extraction splitter has one inlet and two outlets : the main outlet and the extracted flow outlet (ext). Inside, it is made of two BasicTransportModel.
