Basic Transport Model

This model is the basis for most MML components. It has the basic description of a general component with some medium flowing from the inlet connector to the outlet connector. and the way each physical quantity is connected.

Mass Flow Rate

Symbol : QQQ﻿ (caution : in TSP handbook, the mass flow rate is m˙\dot{m}m˙﻿ and the volume flow rate is qqq﻿)

MML symbol : Q

The inlet flow Q_in is equal to the flow of the inlet connector, the outlet flow Q_out is equal to the flow of the outlet connector. In MML, the flow is positive if it comes inside the component and negative if it flows out of the component. Therefore the mean flow Q is (Q_in-Q_out)/2.

Pressure

Symbol : PPP﻿

MML symbol : P

The inlet and outlet pressures are respectively equal to the pressure of the inlet and outlet connectors. The mean pressure P inside the component is the mean of the inlet and outlet pressures.

Enthalpy

Symbol : hhh﻿

MML symbol : h

If the physical flow is from the inlet to the outlet (Q>0) then the inlet enthalpy is equal to the h_vol of the inlet connector (See Connector for a definition of h_vol), so C_in.H = Q_in * h_in = Q_in * C_in.h_vol.

If the flow is reversed (Q<0) then the outlet enthalpy is equal to the h_vol of the outlet connector, so C_out.H = Q_out * h_out = Q_out * C_out.h_vol.

In both cases, by definition, C_out.H = Q_out * h_out and C_in.H = Q_in * h_in by definition of H.

This method allows for the flow to be reversed in a component, however it cannot handle a null flow.

Mass Fractions and species flow rates

Symbols : xix_ixi​﻿ (mass fraction of species i) and QiQ_iQi​﻿ (mass flow rate of species i)

MML symbols : Xi and Qi

For each species, the inlet (outlet) flow rate is equal to the flow rate of the species of the inlet (outlet) connecter.

As for the enthalpy, the mass fractions depend of the direction of the flow. If the flow is from the inlet to the outlet, then the mass fractions are equal to the mass fractions of the inlet connector. If the flow is reversed, then the mass fractions are equal to the mass fractions of the outlet connector. This model cannot handle a null flow rate.

Fluid thermodynamics properties

Symbols : TTT﻿ (temperature) ρ\rhoρ﻿ (density)

MML symbols : T and rho

With the pressure and enthalpy (and mass fractions) at the inlet and outlet, the model computes the thermodynamic state of the medium and can then access the temperatures and densitys.
